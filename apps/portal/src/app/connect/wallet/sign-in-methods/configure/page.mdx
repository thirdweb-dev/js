# Sign-In Methods

thirdweb supports a bunch of various traditional authentication methods. 

From social logins via the [OAuth 2.0 Protocol](https://oauth.net/2/), to email and SMS, to passkeys.

For all login methods, thirdweb generates a non-custodial [wallet](/connect/wallet/security) behind the scenes. Thirdweb wallets enable your users to interact with your application, as well as store tokens and assets they receive while interacting with your application.

## Interacting with your App

Once a user signed in and their wallet is generated, you can use the **[Wallet Connection functions](/typescript/v5/wallets)** to get information about the connected wallet like getting the address, account, and more.
