# Billing & Plan Upgrades

### Learn how to manage your thirdweb plan

thirdweb provides scalable solutions for both individual developers and large-scale enterprise teams, offering plans that adapt and grow in line with your evolving needs.

[Learn more about what is included in our different plans.](https://thirdweb.com/pricing)

---

## Upgrade your plan

To upgrade your account to the **Growth** or **Pro** tier, ensure you have first added billing information to your account through the [Billing](https://thirdweb.com/team/~/~/settings/billing) settings.

### Growth Tier

To upgrade your plan to Growth tier:

<Steps>

<Step title="Billing Settings"> Navigate to Billing settings and select `Get Started`</Step>
<Step title="Confirm UI"> Your account will now reflect limits set by the Growth tier. </Step>

</Steps>

### Pro

To upgrade your plan to Pro tier:

<Steps>

<Step title="Account Information"> Note down your account information including name and email address. </Step>
<Step title="Contact Sales"> [Contact our Sales team](https://thirdweb.com/contact-us) with the provided information through the Billing or Pricing page.</Step>

Our team will reach out to upgrade your account.

</Steps>

## Add-ons

### Engine Cloud-host

You can add-on **cloud-host** to any plan for $99 / month.

To add on cloud-host:

<Steps>

<Step title="Engine Dashboard">Navigate to the Engine dashboard. </Step>
<Step title="Create Instance"> Select “Create Instance” and choose “cloud-host”</Step>

Our engineering team will upgrade your Engine instance and reach out with next steps.

</Steps>

---

## Downgrade your plan

To downgrade your plan from the Growth tier:

<Steps>

<Step title="Billing">Navigate to the billing page.</Step>

<Step title="Downgrade Plan"> Select the `Downgrade` button at the bottom of the Starter plan section.</Step>

    <DocImage src={growthPlan} />


<Step title="Confirm">Select a reason for downgrading and confirm `Downgrade to Starter`</Step>

    <DocImage src={confirmDowngrade} />

The changes will be reflected on your account and in the Billing page interface, shortly. You will also receive an email confirming you have downgraded.

</Steps>
